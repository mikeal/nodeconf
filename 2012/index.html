NodeConf 2012 Summer, Portland

NodeConf Reverse Call for Speakers

Like JSConf, NodeConf will be doing a "reverse" call for speakers. Rather than ask people to submit their talks we're asking the potential audience what they would like to see. You can suggest a person, or a talk, or both. You can even propose something you'd like to present yourself.
