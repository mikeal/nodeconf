The first day will cover "the world of node". You'll hear about node's wide adoption and what has made it such a success across different use cases.

The second day will be "the power of node". We'll cover the largest deployments, bleeding edge debugging and hardware support, and see just how far you can go with node.js.

Tickets & Hotel Information

The official conference hotel is The Jupiter. We have rented the entire hotel, all the guest rooms as well as event space, for NodeConf.

The first batch of tickets sold out with all the rooms in the Jupiter attached. When booking a hotel room in Portland you should try to find one in the Southeast as close as you can to the Jupiter Hotel.

General Tickets go on sale at 10am Pacific time on Monday, May 7th and will be available here. The first batch of tickets sold out in under 10 minutes so if you're excited about NodeConf you'll want to be prepared.
