NodeConf SummerCamp Sponsorship Prospectus

Want to be a NodeConf SummerCamp sponsor?

This year sponsorship is limited to 6 participants who will be exclusively featured on the site and associated with SummerCamp items and events.

Sponsorship includes:

  • Single identified sponsor of one of the SummerCamp events.
    2 parties and 6 meals to choose from. Selection is first-come, first-served.
  • 4 "Semi-Private" tickets.
    Highest end tickets we are selling for the conference. Includes "semi-private" accomodations, 3 meals, etc.
  • 200 x 200 logo on event website.
  • 400 words on the sponsor page.
  • Insert and/or swag at registration.
  • One (1) display banner (you provide) that will remain up during the entire conference.

Because we've limited the number of sponsors there is only one sponsorship level with a total cost of 6K. Cost is non-negotiable.
